# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** UNav  
**Developer:** AI4CE Lab, New York University  
**Last Updated:** August 2025  

---

## 1. Introduction
This privacy policy explains how our application (“UNav”) handles user data and permissions.  
We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding how your data is used.

---

## 2. Permissions We Use

### Camera (`android.permission.CAMERA`)
- **Purpose:** The camera is used exclusively for real-time localization and navigation functions.  
- **Data Handling:**  
  - Images are processed **locally on the device** or sent securely to our research server for navigation purposes.  
  - We **do not store, sell, or share** camera images with third parties.  
- **Why It’s Needed:** Without camera access, the app cannot provide visual navigation assistance.

---

## 3. Data Collection and Usage
- We **do not collect personally identifiable information** unless you voluntarily provide it.  
- Navigation and localization data may be processed for research and system improvement.  
- Any collected data is used strictly for academic research and user experience enhancement.

---

## 4. Data Sharing
- We **do not sell or share** your personal data with advertisers or third parties.  
- Data may be shared only with trusted research collaborators under strict agreements.

---

## 5. User Control
- You can disable the camera permission at any time via your device settings, though core navigation features will not function without it.  
- You may also request deletion of any data we have collected (if applicable).

---

## 6. Security
- All communication with servers is encrypted (HTTPS).  
- We follow NYU IT and MCIT guidelines for data security and compliance.
